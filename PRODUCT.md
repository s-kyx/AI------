# 先做一点

一个把脑中杂乱事情轻轻拆成下一步的 AI 待办应用。

## Register

product

## Users

Young office workers and students who feel anxious because many small and large tasks are mixed together in their head. They come to the page when they do not want to learn a workflow system or fill a complicated form.

## Product Purpose

The product turns one free-form worry dump into calm advice and a dated, time-based to-do list using DeepSeek deep thinking. Success means the user feels less tense within the first screen and knows the next few things to do.

## Brand Personality

Calm, simple, tender. The interface should feel white, clean, quiet, lightly cute, and emotionally reassuring.

## Anti-references

It should not look like a task-management dashboard, a productivity control panel, a complex GTD app, or a dense SaaS screen. Avoid heavy metrics, priority matrices, harsh colors, aggressive copy, and anything that makes the user feel judged for being disorganized.

## Design Principles

- One big input first: the user should only need to type what is bothering them.
- Emotional safety before efficiency: the page should lower tension before asking for action.
- Model handles complexity: users should not manually score, tag, or estimate tasks.
- Calm motion: use slow light, breathing, and soft feedback instead of busy animation.
- Cute but restrained: small expressions can comfort the user without turning the tool into a toy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use readable contrast, clear focus states, keyboard-friendly controls, and reduced-motion fallbacks. Motion should never be required to understand or use the page.
